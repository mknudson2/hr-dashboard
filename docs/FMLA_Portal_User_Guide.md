## Introduction

The FMLA Self-Service Portal is a secure web application that allows employees to manage their Family and Medical Leave Act (FMLA) time tracking and enables supervisors to review and approve their team's FMLA submissions.

### Key Features

**For Employees:**
- View your active FMLA cases and remaining hours
- Submit time entries for supervisor approval
- Request new FMLA leave
- Track the status of your submissions

**For Supervisors:**
- View your team's FMLA status at a glance
- Review and approve/reject time submissions
- Modify time entries when needed (with documented reason)
- Export team FMLA reports

---

## Getting Started

### Accessing the Portal

1. Open your web browser and navigate to the FMLA Portal URL provided by your HR department
2. You will see the login page

### Logging In

1. Enter your **username** (the same credentials you use for other company systems)
2. Enter your **password**
3. Click **Sign In**
4. If two-factor authentication is enabled, enter the code from your authenticator app

### Understanding Your Dashboard

After logging in, you'll see your personalized dashboard. What you see depends on your role:

- **Employees** see their own FMLA cases, hours used, and recent submissions
- **Supervisors** see both their personal FMLA information (if applicable) AND their team's FMLA overview
- **Supervisor-only accounts** (without personal FMLA cases) see only team management features

## For Employees

### Viewing Your FMLA Cases

Navigate to **My Cases** in the sidebar to see all your FMLA cases.

**What You'll See:**
- **Case Number** - Unique identifier for your FMLA case
- **Leave Type** - The type of FMLA leave (Employee Medical, Family Care, etc.)
- **Status** - Current status (Active, Approved, Pending, Closed)
- **Date Range** - Start and end dates of your approved leave
- **Hours Approved** - Total FMLA hours approved for this case
- **Hours Used** - Hours you've already taken
- **Hours Remaining** - Hours still available

**Rolling 12-Month Summary:**
At the top of the page, you'll see your rolling 12-month FMLA usage summary, which shows how many of your 480 FMLA hours you've used in the past year.

---

### Submitting Time Entries

When you take FMLA time, you need to submit it for your supervisor's approval.

**Steps to Submit Time:**

1. Click **Submit Time** in the sidebar
2. **Select your FMLA case** from the dropdown (only active cases are shown)
3. **Choose the date** of the leave
4. **Enter the hours** you're requesting (up to 24 hours per day)
5. **Select the entry type:**
   - **Full Day** - A complete workday of FMLA leave
   - **Partial Day** - Less than a full day
   - **Intermittent** - For intermittent leave schedules
6. **Add notes** (optional) - Provide any additional context for your supervisor
7. Click **Submit for Approval**

**After Submitting:**
- Your submission will show as "Pending" until your supervisor reviews it
- You'll be redirected to My Submissions where you can track its status
- Once approved, the hours will be deducted from your available balance

**Important Notes:**
- Submit your time promptly after taking FMLA leave
- Ensure you select the correct FMLA case if you have multiple
- Your supervisor will be notified of pending submissions

---

### Requesting New FMLA Leave

If you need to start a new FMLA case (not submit time for an existing case), use the Request Leave feature.

**Steps to Request Leave:**

1. Click **Request Leave** in the sidebar
2. **Select the leave type:**
   - Employee Medical - For your own serious health condition
   - Family Care - To care for a family member
   - Parental Leave - For birth, adoption, or foster care
   - Military Family - For qualifying military exigencies
   - Military Caregiver - To care for a servicemember
3. **Enter your requested start date**
4. **Enter your expected end date** (if known)
5. **Specify if the leave is:**
   - **Intermittent** - Taken in separate blocks of time
   - **Reduced Schedule** - Working fewer hours per day/week
6. **Provide a reason** (optional but recommended)
7. **Estimate hours per week** (for intermittent/reduced schedule)
8. Click **Submit Request**

**What Happens Next:**
- Your request is sent to HR for review
- HR will contact you regarding required documentation
- Once approved, an FMLA case will be created for you
- You'll then be able to submit time against that case

---

### Tracking Your Submissions

Navigate to **My Submissions** to see all your time entry submissions.

**Submission Statuses:**
- **Pending** (Yellow) - Awaiting supervisor review
- **Approved** (Green) - Approved as submitted
- **Revised** (Blue) - Approved with modified hours
- **Rejected** (Red) - Not approved

**Filtering:**
Use the filter buttons to view only certain statuses (All, Pending, Approved, Rejected, Revised).

**Understanding Revised Submissions:**
If your supervisor revised your submission, you'll see:
- Your original requested hours
- The approved hours (may be different)
- Reviewer notes explaining the change

## For Supervisors

### Team Dashboard Overview

As a supervisor, your dashboard shows an overview of your team's FMLA status.

**Key Metrics:**
- **Team Size** - Number of employees you supervise
- **Team Members on FMLA** - Employees with active FMLA cases
- **Pending Submissions** - Time entries awaiting your review

**Quick Actions:**
- Review pending submissions directly from the dashboard
- Navigate to detailed team views

---

### Reviewing Pending Submissions

Navigate to **Pending Reviews** to see all submissions awaiting your approval.

**For Each Submission, You'll See:**
- Employee name and department
- FMLA case number
- Date of the leave
- Hours requested
- Entry type (Full Day, Partial Day, Intermittent)
- Employee's notes
- When it was submitted

**Reviewing a Submission:**

1. Click **Review** on any pending submission
2. A review panel will open showing full details
3. Choose your action:
   - **Approve** - Accept the submission as-is
   - **Reject** - Deny the submission
   - **Modify** - Approve with different hours
4. **Enter your reason** (required for all actions)
5. If modifying, enter the approved hours
6. Click **Submit Review**

---

### Approving, Rejecting, or Modifying Entries

**Approving:**
- The employee's requested hours will be deducted from their FMLA balance
- A leave entry will be recorded in the FMLA system

**Rejecting:**
- The hours will NOT be deducted
- Provide a clear reason so the employee understands
- The employee may need to resubmit with corrections

**Modifying (Revising):**
- Use this when the hours need adjustment
- Enter the correct number of hours
- Provide a reason for the modification
- The modified hours (not original) will be deducted

**Important:**
- All your actions are logged in the audit trail
- Reasons are required and will be visible to the employee
- You can only review submissions from your direct reports

---

### Exporting Reports

Navigate to **Reports** to export your team's FMLA data.

**Steps to Export:**

1. Select the **date range** for your report
2. Choose the **format** (CSV)
3. Click **Export Report**

**Report Contents:**
- Employee ID and name
- Department
- Case number
- Leave dates
- Hours requested and approved
- Entry types
- Submission status
- Review dates and notes

**Use Cases:**
- Monthly FMLA tracking reports
- Payroll reconciliation
- Audit documentation

## Frequently Asked Questions

### For Employees

**Q: How quickly will my submission be reviewed?**
A: Your supervisor receives notification of pending submissions. Review times vary, but most submissions are processed within 1-2 business days.

**Q: What if my submission is rejected?**
A: Review the rejection reason provided by your supervisor. Make any necessary corrections and resubmit, or contact your supervisor for clarification.

**Q: Can I edit a submission after submitting it?**
A: No, once submitted, you cannot edit it. If you made an error, contact your supervisor before they review it, or wait for it to be processed and submit a correction.

**Q: What's the difference between submitting time and requesting leave?**
A:
- **Submit Time** = Log hours against an existing, approved FMLA case
- **Request Leave** = Ask HR to start a new FMLA case

**Q: Why can't I see the Submit Time option?**
A: You may not have any active FMLA cases. If you believe you should, contact HR.

### For Supervisors

**Q: Can I see FMLA cases for employees outside my team?**
A: No. You can only view and manage FMLA submissions for your direct reports.

**Q: What if I need to change a submission after I've already reviewed it?**
A: Contact HR. Once a submission is reviewed, it cannot be changed through the portal.

**Q: Are my review decisions audited?**
A: Yes. All supervisor actions are logged with timestamps, IP addresses, and the reasons you provide. This audit trail is maintained for compliance purposes.

**Q: What if an employee reports to multiple supervisors?**
A: The system matches employees to supervisors based on the supervisor field in their employee record. Contact HR if changes are needed.
