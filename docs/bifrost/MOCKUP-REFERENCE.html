Hi Michael! I'm Mímir, your HR knowledge assistant. I can help with questions about benefits, policies, time off, payroll, and more. What can I help with?
What's my dental coverage for a root canal?
Based on your Dental — Standard plan, a root canal falls under "Major Services" which are covered at 50% after your deductible. Your annual deductible is $50 (individual), and your annual maximum benefit is $1,500.

If the root canal costs $800, you'd pay approximately $425 after the deductible.
📄 Benefits Plan Summary (SPD) — Dental, p. 12
Is there a waiting period for major services?
No waiting period on your plan. Since you enrolled during open enrollment and have been on the plan for over 12 months, all services including major dental work are fully accessible.
📄 Benefits Plan Summary (SPD) — Dental, p. 8
